BRIEF OVERVIEW
Dr. Shrivastava’s leadership is characterized by creativity, dynamic problem-solving, and a commitment to enhancing both
academic and co-curricular growth at Parul University and beyond.
Dr. Ruchi Srivastava is an empathetic individual who believes in putting service above self for the betterment of society. Her
dedication extends beyond her role at the Rotary Club of Baroda Greens; she also serves as the President of the Women
Development Cell at Parul University. In this capacity, she is deeply committed to promoting gender equality and empowering
women. Her work reflects a genuine passion for creating positive social change and fostering an inclusive environment.
Dr. Ruchi Shrivastava is a distinguished leader and academician with a career spanning over 25 years. She began her career
as a Lecturer in the Department of Civil Engineering in 1997 and was elevated to Principal in 2013. Her leadership at Parul
University is marked by significant contributions to placement training, industry-institute interaction, and various educational
initiatives
